02
Notes field
Sometimes the worker needs to say something: the truck arrived late, 2 items were already on site, the customer wasn't present. Where does that note live? On the count screen, or only when reviewing before confirming?
help
Decision needed: Is a notes field needed at all? If yes — does it belong on the count screen (always visible) or on the review screen (less distracting)?

03
Offline behaviour
Yard workers often have no signal inside warehouse buildings. The question is: can they still count and confirm a shipment without internet, and have it sync when they walk back into signal range? These three screens show the full offline journey — no signal, counting offline, and syncing when signal returns.
help
Decision needed: Does the app need to work offline, or can we require a connection to confirm? Offline support is significantly more work to build — worth confirming with the dev team.
